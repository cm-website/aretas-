Who Are We

The name 'Aretas,' derived from an Ancient Greek term for 'excellence,' embodies the mission of the organization to deliver top-notch education in ancient studies to the masses. Aretas emphasizes accessibility, community, and engagement (A.C.E.), offering a unique learning opportunity for individuals to delve into Roman and Greek classics without any cost or device limitations. Serving as a web-based platform, Aretas can be accessed seamlessly through various electronic devices. The organization is distinguished by its commitment to crafting educational content that is both fun and scholarly, particularly targeting the curiosity of younger learners.

Our Services

To revolutionize classical education by making it accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or means, through a platform that delivers expert knowledge in a captivating and interactive manner.

Beginning Latin Seminars

An interactive journey through Ancient Rome and Greece, allowing users to explore significant historical stories and engage with cultural interests, fostering a deeper understanding and excitement for classical studies.

Educational Mini-Games

An interactive service that immerses users in games set with ancient Greece and Rome knowledge, allowing them to engage with historical figures and pivotal events while honing their understanding of classical studies.

Chatting with Fellow Scholars

Engage in discussions with experts and peers, bringing Ancient Greek and Roman worlds to life through real-time dialogue and Socratic discussions, uniquely fostering curiosity and involvement among younger audiences.
